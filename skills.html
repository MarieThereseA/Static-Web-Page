All About Makeup

If you can't tell, I love makeup. On this page I'll showcase my makeup routine.

  1. Prime your face (t-zone + nose)
  2. Color correct where needed
  3. Place and blend Contour
  4. Use a light brush and place foundation everywhere
  5. PLace and blend liquid blush
  6. Do brows
  7. Blend concealer
  8. Use baking powder to bake concealer (let sit)
  9. Use Bronzer over contour
  10. Use Powder blush over liquid blush
  11. Use powder foundation over areas that haven't been powdered yet
  12. Set face with first setting spray
  13. Set t-zone with translucent setting powder
  14. Mascara
  15. Eyeliner
  16. Lip liner
  17. Highlight nose
  18. Highlight cheekbones
  19. Set face with second setting spray
  20. Lashes (optional)
  21. And you're done!
